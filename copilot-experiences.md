# Copilot Chat: 
A conversational AI interface for general questions, coding help, web search, etc. it is used for quick tasks. 
# Copilot for Business (Microsoft 365 Copilot): 
AI embedded in Microsoft 365 apps (Word, Excel, Teams, etc.) for work productivity. This is used for tasks like drafting emails, summarizing docs, or creating presentations within your Microsoft apps.
# Copilot Studio: 
This is a platform to build custom copilots (AI assistants) for specific workflows, websites, Teams, etc. It is used if you need a tailored AI for your business processes or custom services.
